陳俊瑋

Hi there,
I'm currently a junior majoring in Electrical Engineering, and also a passionate designer. My design journey was sparked by a deep enthusiasm for certain anime and TV series. I was inspired to create my own works based on these shows, which led me to embark on a self-taught journey to learn Photoshop and Illustrator.

The inspiration for my work naturally stems from anime and TV series. These fictional worlds serve as the cornerstone of my design expedition, driving me to acquire the skills necessary to craft unique wallpapers, avatars, and more. I really enjoy translating these inspirations into visual designs, crafting those unique and captivating artworks.

Feel free to explore my portfolio. I hope that my design pieces inspire you and provide you with delightful creative experiences.
